Your Columbia Connection

Since 1901, the Columbia University Club of New York has been a social, intellectual, cultural, recreational, and professional center of activity for its members. A private social club for alumni of the eighteen schools and divisions of Columbia University, Barnard College, Teachers College, and affiliate schools, the Club offers a gracious and welcoming environment conveniently located in two blocks from Grand Central Terminal.

The benefits of membership include:

  1. Full-service club facility in Midtown. This includes dining, conference and meeting rooms , catering and banqueting, overnight accommodations, lounge and athletic facilities.
  2. A sense of community through shared activities and interests
  3. Events that fulfill a variety of interests, both at the Club and beyond
  4. Gracious and welcoming staff
  5. Access to over 200 reciprocal clubs worldwide
