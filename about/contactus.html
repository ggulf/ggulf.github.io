Columbia Undergraduate Membership*

Undergraduate membership has benefits. Join now and we will waive your initiation fee and apply a $50 credit to your house account.

As long as you use your $50.00 club credit, your membership pays for itself.

What’s available for $50 and under?

Join us for a specialty cocktail – $14
Center yourself with a Yoga class – $15
Dine on Ponzu Salmon – $29
Get ready for the holidays with a Personal Training session (30 minutes) – $38
Wine with dinner? Sauvignon Sancerre, 2013 – $47
Just need to decompress? Massage (30 minute) – $38

*Undergraduate dues are not prorated and cannot be paid monthly. $50 credits are good through June of your graduation year.

Columbia University Club Referral Program

Membership in the Columbia University Club of New York is all about building community and expanding your network. We want you to be an active part of creating a thriving network of your fellow alumni. Introducing friends and family to the Columbia University Club can be a rewarding experience in itself, but we found a way to thank you for spreading the word.

Encourage a friend or family member to become a member today. If your referral joins the Columbia University Club we will reward you with a gift certificate to The Grill. Two friends join – two gift certificates for you.

Gift certificate amounts are decided by graduation year of the recommended new member.

  • $20 gift certificate for a new member 1-7 years out of their undergraduate institution (2007-Present)
  • $40 gift certificate for a new member 8-14 years out of their undergraduate institution (2000-2006)
  • $50 gift certificate for a new member 15+ years out of their undergraduate institution (1999-Earlier)

To make it easy for anyone to join, pass along the following link:
http://www.columbiaclub.org/join/apply-now/

Please remember to tell your friend or family member to let us know that you referred them for membership. We will notify you by e-mail when your referral joins.

Questions? E-mail membership@columbiaclub.org.

*This offer is only available to members in good standing. Suspended, expelled, and resigned members are not eligible to participate.
